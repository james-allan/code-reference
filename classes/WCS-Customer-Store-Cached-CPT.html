woocommerce-subscriptions/vendor/subscriptions-core/includes/data-stores/class-wcs-customer-store-cached-cpt.php : 18

Customer data store for subscriptions stored in Custom Post Types, with caching.

Adds a persistent caching layer on top of WCS_Customer_Store_CPT for more performant queries to find a user's subscriptions.

Cache is based on the current blog in case of a multisite environment.
