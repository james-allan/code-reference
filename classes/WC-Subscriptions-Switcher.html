add_line_item_meta()

woocommerce-subscriptions/includes/switching/class-wc-subscriptions-switcher.php : 855

Store switch related data on the line item on the subscription and switch order.

public static add_line_item_meta(WC_Order_Item_Product $order_item, string $cart_item_key, array<string|int, mixed> $cart_item, WC_Order $order) : mixed

For subscriptions: items on a new billing schedule are left to be added as new subscriptions, but we also want to keep a record of them being a switch, so we do that here.

For orders: to prorate sign-up fee and recurring amounts correctly when the customer switches a subscription multiple times, keep a record of the amount for each on the order item.

New subscriptions will be created when:

  • The current subscription has more than 1 line item @see self::is_single_item_subscription() and
