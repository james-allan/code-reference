Ensure customers can cancel early renewal orders.

public static add_cancel_order_action(array<string|int, mixed> $actions, WC_Order $order) : array<string|int, mixed>

Renewal orders are usually not cancellable because @see WCS_Cart_Renewal::filter_my_account_my_orders_actions() prevents it. In the case of early renewals, the customer has opted for early renewal and so should be able to cancel it in order to reactivate their subscription.

Allow customers to cancel early renewal orders from their account page.

public static allow_early_renewal_order_cancellation() : mixed

Renewal orders are usually not cancellable because @see WC_Subscriptions_Renewal_Order::prevent_cancelling_renewal_orders() prevents the request from being processed. In the case of early renewals, the customer has opted for early renewal and so should be able to cancel it in order to reactivate their subscription.
